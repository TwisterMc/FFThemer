# Code Of Conduct

Participation in this project requires professional and respectful behavior.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, constructive, and technically specific.
- Assume good intent, but challenge ideas with evidence.
- Keep discussions focused on the code, product, and user impact.
- Respect maintainers' time by searching existing context before raising repeat questions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory or degrading language
- Deliberate disruption of discussions or reviews
- Publishing private or sensitive information without permission

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that violates this policy and may restrict participation for repeated or severe violations.

For concerns about conduct, contact the maintainers privately through the repository contact channels.
